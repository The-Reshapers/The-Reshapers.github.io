Madrid Workation Program

Come to Madrid to establish your operation hub in Europe, hire the best talent, find the best providers, build your business network, visit Silicon River and enjoy one of the most exciting capitals of the world. Our two week program will take your business to the next level.

A Typical day

Every morning participants holds a standup meeting to share goals for the day as well as insights and achievements from previous days. Then engage in scheduled activities that includes:

  • 1-on-1 meetings with mentors
  • Training sessions
  • Visits to special venues
  • Hiring interviews
